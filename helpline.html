Helpline & Support

Get help and find answers to your questions.

In Case of a Medical Emergency

For immediate, life-threatening situations, please dial:

112 (National Emergency Number)

Kerala Health Helpline (DISHA)

For health information, services, and support, call:

1056 Call Now

District-Level Contacts

Frequently Asked Questions

You can schedule a new appointment by clicking the "My Appointments" tab in the sidebar, and then clicking the "Schedule New Appointment" button. You will be able to select a hospital, department, and available time slot.

You can download a digital copy of your Health ID card anytime from your dashboard. If you need a physical card, please visit the nearest Primary Health Centre (PHC) for assistance.

Consultations and many basic services at government health centers are provided free of charge or at a highly subsidized rate. Please inquire at the specific hospital for details about tests and specialized treatments.
